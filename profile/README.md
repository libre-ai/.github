# Libre AI

**Sovereign, explainable software — built in the open, by AI agents under human governance.**

Libre AI is an independent open-source software lab. We are rebuilding a full product line for the AI era from a clean base, on one simple conviction: tools that matter should belong to the people who use them. Open code, data under your control, traceable decisions, reversibility — not marketing promises, but written, public, verifiable rules.

## An unusual factory

Most software companies show their products and hide their factory. Here, the factory is the first product.

The entire portfolio is built by AI agents operating under a governed method: plans bounded before acting, proof required at every step, control gates where a human decides, documented refusals when a limit is reached. Nothing happens backstage — every pull request, every architecture decision, every gate passed is visible in this forge, which documents itself.

That method is called **Polaris**. It drives the construction of everything else — and it will become a product in its own right, for teams that want to operate their own agent fleets with the same level of rigor.

## Where we stand — no embellishment

**Nothing is released yet.** Rather than build on top of the past, we froze our historical repositories and started clean: coherent architecture, final names, quality gates in place from the first line. The foundations — contracts, architecture, shared building blocks — are taking shape in the central repository, [`libre-ai/libre-ai`](https://github.com/libre-ai/libre-ai), where all current work happens. Every product already has its reserved repository, whose README says what it is, what already exists and where the work lives — as does the future public website, [Website](https://github.com/libre-ai/website).

One rule governs our communication: nothing goes in the shop window that cannot be verified. Gates passed and evidence are published as we go; the organization's complete inventory, [`ecosystem/repositories.v1.yaml`](https://github.com/libre-ai/libre-ai/blob/main/ecosystem/repositories.v1.yaml), is the machine-readable source of which this page is the human reading.

## Our commitments

- **Sovereignty** — open source end to end, no proprietary lock-in, guaranteed reversibility.
- **Privacy** — local-first wherever possible; zero profiling, zero personal data in logs.
- **Explainability** — every selection, every verdict, every refusal can be traced back to its justification.
- **Proof before promise** — a capability is claimed only once it can be publicly demonstrated.
